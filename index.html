How the CC Score is calculated

CC Score is a 0–100 composite across four attributable KPIs. Each component is hard-capped at its weight — exceeding the standard earns no bonus. 100/100 means meeting every standard simultaneously.

40 pts — Call answer rate · standard ≥70% · earns (actual ÷ 70) × 40, capped at 40
30 pts — Add-on attach rate · standard ≥44% · earns (actual ÷ 44) × 30, capped at 30
20 pts — ACE scorecard · standard ≥8.0 · earns (actual ÷ 8.0) × 20, capped at 20
10 pts — Avg booking ticket · baseline $700 · earns (actual ÷ 700) × 10, capped at 10
ACE baselines are pending recalibration — new scorecard questions went live June 2026. Scores shown use the prior baseline and are labeled accordingly.
Workload context is shown alongside every agent score. A performance gap from a high-volume agent has more business impact than the same gap from a low-volume one.
