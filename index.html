How the CC Score is calculated

CC Score is a composite metric across five attributable KPIs. Scores are uncapped — exceeding a standard earns proportional bonus points. 100 = every standard met simultaneously.

35 pts — Call answer rate · standard ≥70% · (actual ÷ 70) × 35
20 pts — Miss streak rate · standard ≤2/wk · min((2 ÷ actual) × 20, 20); full 20 pts at 0 streaks
20 pts — Add-on attach rate · standard ≥44% · (actual ÷ 44) × 20
15 pts — ACE scorecard · standard ≥8.0 · (actual ÷ 8.0) × 15
10 pts — Avg booking ticket · baseline $700 · (actual ÷ 700) × 10

Tiers: Exceptional = score ≥100 AND ≤1 component below standard · Strong = score 90–99 AND fewer than 3 below · Monitor = score 80–89 OR 3+ below · Needs Attention = score below 80
