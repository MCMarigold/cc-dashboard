How the CC Score is calculated

CC Score is a composite metric across five attributable KPIs. Scores are uncapped — exceeding a standard earns proportional bonus points. 100 = meeting every standard simultaneously. Scores above 100 reflect overperformance.

35 pts — Call answer rate · standard ≥70% · earns (actual ÷ 70) × 35, uncapped
20 pts — Miss streak rate · standard ≤2/wk · earns min((2 ÷ actual) × 20, 20); capped at 20; full 20 pts at 0 streaks
20 pts — Add-on attach rate · standard ≥44% · earns (actual ÷ 44) × 20, uncapped
15 pts — ACE scorecard · standard ≥8.0 · earns (actual ÷ 8.0) × 15, uncapped
10 pts — Avg booking ticket · baseline $700 · earns (actual ÷ 700) × 10, uncapped

Tiers: Exceptional = score ≥100 AND ≤1 below standard · Strong = score ≥90 AND <3 below standard · Monitor = score 80–89 OR 3+ below standard · Needs Attention = score <80
